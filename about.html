About

Hey, I'm Logan, a software developer currently studying Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo, Canada.

I am passionate about programming projects and learning new skills, often taking on larger projects to learn a new programming language, tool, or creating something meaningful that people can use.

Currently, I'm most experienced in C-based languages, with over 3+ years of experience starting back in November 2021. Between 2022 and 2024, I developed over 40 projects in C#, as well as programmed for a VEX Robotics team in C++ and experiemented with Python and Java.

Most recently, I've been learning web development, teaching myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript as well as JQuery and React (used for this website!). In my current project, I'm working with Tailwind CSS and NextJS, and also plan on learning Typescript in the near future to futher expand my web-dev skillset and create websites more effectively.

I hope you enjoy my website and portfolio! It was made with only the JQuery library, as I wanted to teach myself pure CSS/HTML.
